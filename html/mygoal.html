I believe that taking care of the earth means taking care of myself.

Use Green Energy

I believe that using green energy is the next step towards sustainable development. I try to use solar power and energy saving gadgets which is cool and helping the environment. It also cut down my electricity bill, water bill etc. So helping the environment is helping yourself.

Plant tree once in a while

I always try to plant trees on my birthday every year. It starts when I was 5 when my parents thought that it will be memorable till I grow old. So I always try to keep the tradition alive. The tree I planted in 5 was so big right now that it makes me feel a lot of nostalgia. You should try it too.

Recycle what you can

I think the main goal of recycling is no more extraction of natural resources. So I try to make a lot of effort for recycling even though, I cannot recycle myself. I try to make a separate trash bag for plastic, bio waste and biodegradable and metal cans. And I try to my best not to buy new things and try to fix old things if the old things broke down. If I cannot use it, I try to give it away to someone who can use it.
